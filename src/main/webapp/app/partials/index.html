Aleksandr Sergeyevich Pushkin (rus. Aleksandr Sergeyevich Pushkin) (26 maya (6 iyunya) 1799-29 yanvarya (10 fevralya) 1837) - russkiy poet, dramaturg i prozaik, reformator i sozdatel' sovremennogo russkogo literaturnogo yazyka, avtor kriticheskikh i istoricheskikh proizvedeniy. Otnositsya k vidneyshikh russkikh pisateley, yego teksty rassmatrivayutsya kak prestizhnyy obrazets russkoy literatury, podobno tekstam Dante v Italii ili Gete v Germanii. Pushkina yeshche pri zhizni nazyvali geniyem, i so vtoroy poloviny 1820-kh godov on stal schitat'sya «pervym russkim poetom» (ne tol'ko sovremennosti, no i vsekh vremen), a vokrug yego lichnosti u chitatel'skoy publiki sozdalsya nastoyashchiy kul't.
